Topic 15

Volunteer Application

Dear Nguyen Van A,

I am writing to offer to work as a part-time volunteer in your organization. I want to apply because I love children and I want to help them have a better life. I think this work is very meaningful.

I am suitable for this position because I am kind, patient, and friendly. I can talk to children, play with them, and help them with simple things. I also work well in a team.

I am free in the evenings and at weekends. I hope to hear from you soon.

Best regards,

Topic 16

Housewarming Invitation

Dear Nguyen Van A,

My name is Minh, and I have just moved into the house next to yours. I want to say hello and introduce myself to you.

This Saturday evening, I am planning to have a small housewarming party with some friends and family. We will eat, talk, and listen to a little music. The party will start at 7 p.m. and finish before 10 p.m. I will try to keep the noise low.

I would like to invite you to come to the party. I hope you can join us.

Best regards,

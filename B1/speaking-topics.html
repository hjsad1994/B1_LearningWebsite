Topic 9

Describe the job or career you have or hope to have

1. What is the job?

The job I want is to be a teacher, which I think is a very meaningful profession.

2. What does it involve?

A teacher teaches students as well as helps them learn. In addition, they guide students in life.

3. Why do you choose it?

I choose this job because I like children along with teaching. Therefore, I feel happy when I help them learn new things.

4. What should you do to be successful with it?

I should study hard, speak well, and be kind, since these are important qualities of a good teacher.

Topic 10

Describe a movie that you particularly like

1. What is the name of the movie?

The movie is Titanic, which is very famous all over the world.

2. Who stars in the movie?

The stars are Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet. Also, they act very well in the movie.

3. What is it about?

It is about love on a big ship; however, the story ends in a very sad way.

4. Why do you particularly like it?

I like it because it is sad, sweet, and very interesting. Besides that, the music is also beautiful.

Topic 11

Describe a wedding you have been to

1. Whose wedding was it?

It was my cousin's wedding, which took place last year.

2. Who attended the wedding?

My family, friends, and relatives came to the wedding, making it very crowded and lively.

3. What did you wear to the wedding?

I wore a shirt with black pants. As a result, I felt quite confident in my outfit.

4. What happened during the wedding?

We ate food, took photos, and gave our best wishes. Overall, everyone had a great time together.

5. How did you feel?

I felt happy as well as excited because it was a special day for my cousin.

Topic 12

Describe an activity you do when you are alone in your free time

1. What activity is it?

It is listening to music, an activity I do almost every day.

2. Is it easy or difficult to do?

It's very easy to do because it's just listening to music, so anyone can enjoy it anytime.

3. How do you feel when you do it?

I feel calm and happy. Moreover, sometimes I forget my stress.

4. Are there any benefits of doing it?

Yes. It helps me relax while making me feel better. Additionally, it improves my mood.

Topic 13

Describe your favorite photograph

1. What photograph is it?

It is a photo of my family, which is very important to me.

2. When and where was it taken?

It was taken two years ago at my grandparents' house during a family gathering.

3. How long have you had it?

I have had it for two years. Nowadays, I keep it very carefully.

4. Why do you like it?

I like it because my family looks happy in it. Also, it reminds me of good memories.

Topic 14

Describe a childhood friend of yours

1. What is his/her name?

His name is Khanh, who is someone I grew up with.

2. What did he/she look like?

He was small with short black hair. Besides, he looked very active.

3. What was he/she like?

He was kind, friendly, and happy. For this reason, everyone around him liked him a lot.

4. Why did you like him/her?

I liked him because he was nice to me; in fact, he always helped me.

5. What did you like to do together?

We played games, rode bikes, and studied together. Looking back, we had a lot of fun.
